⚡ Electric Field Football
Educational Physics Simulation
Created and Conceptualized by
Kajal Thapar

An interactive simulation where students place electric charges to steer a football using electric fields — learning Coulomb's Law, superposition, and Newton's second law through play.
E = kq / r²
F = qE
a = F / m
E_net = Σ Eᵢ
V = kq / r
E = −∇V
Φ = Q/ε₀
Physics Engine: Semi-implicit Euler integration
with softened Coulomb field (ε-regularization)
and field-magnitude clamping for stability.

Grades: Nursery through Grade 12
Levels: 4 progressive challenge levels
